About Us

Skip to main content

Due to the number of students from Asia-pacific region has been increasing every year, so we decided to establish the Asia-Pacific Students association in order for students to come together and cerebrate cultures. Recently, the Association counts more than 70 members – students from Thailand, Laos, Indonesia, Malaysia, Vietnam, India, and Papua New Guinea Etc. But not only native students can join – we are happy to welcome everyone, so please don’t hesitate to join us!

Our actions and goals:

Promoting and strengthening the awareness of Asia-Pacific cultures to everyone. Empowering the students with the attitude of serving their countries willingly Reserving a place for the students to illustrate their talents, by organizing various kinds of activities, such as trips, events, contests, conferences, national days, many more!

Our projects

Celebration of the diversity Days: We are planning to organize the Asia-Pacific cultural days for all students to show their talents. Trips around Poland: Gdańsk, Łódź, Ełk. Cultural days: there will be a cultural days shows from each country for a students to promote their countries to everyone, such as Thailand day, Malaysia day, Vietnam day, and so on.
